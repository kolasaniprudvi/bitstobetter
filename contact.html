Frequently Asked Questions

Does BitsToBetter collect my personal data? ▼

No. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or send any personal information to our servers.

Is BitsToBetter free forever? ▼

Absolutely. BitsToBetter is free and always will be. No hidden fees, no paywalls, no premium tiers.

Can I export my calculator results? ▼

Yes! Many calculators have a "Download Report" button that generates a PDF with your results.
