Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the 🤸 Calisthenics & Gym Posture Guide for medical diagnosis?

No, the 🤸 Calisthenics & Gym Posture Guide is strictly for educational and screening purposes. Always consult a healthcare professional or doctor for medical advice and diagnosis.

How often should I use the 🤸 Calisthenics & Gym Posture Guide to track progress?

For most health metrics, checking weekly or bi-weekly provides a much more accurate trend line than daily checking, which can fluctuate due to hydration and sleep.

Does the 🤸 Calisthenics & Gym Posture Guide account for body composition?

Standard formulas rely on baseline metrics. If you are a highly trained athlete or have atypical body composition, you should rely on clinical measurements rather than standard calculator outputs.
