Employee Provident Fund (EPF)

EPF is a government-backed retirement savings scheme where both employee and employer contribute 12% of basic salary each month. The current EPFO interest rate is 8.25%.

EPF Contribution Structure

  • Employee: 12% of basic salary goes to EPF
  • Employer: 12% of basic salary (3.67% to EPF + 8.33% to EPS)
  • Total: 15.67% of basic salary accumulates in EPF
