Strategic Execution

Efficient amortization is about attacking the principal early when interest comprises the bulk of your payment.

Key Parameters

  • ● Extra Payments: Even a small additional monthly payment directly reduces the principal, compounding your savings exponentially.
  • ● Refinancing: Lowering your rate midway through amortization can reset the clock; ensure the new lower rate outweighs the closing costs.

Myth vs. Fact

The Myth

"I should always stretch my loan to the max tenure for lower payments."

The Fact

Long tenures drastically increase total interest paid. Opting for a slightly higher payment on a shorter tenure saves immense capital.
