Formulas, tax context & strategic parameters

FIRE accumulation rests on corpus = retirement annual spend ÷ Safe Withdrawal Rate. Spending at independence is today’s baseline grown by inflation: ET = E0 × (1 + i)T. Enter inflation and SWR explicitly so stress tests stay transparent.

PMTdue = (FV − PV × (1+r)n) × r / [((1+r)n − 1) × (1+r)]

Contributions at month-start are annuity-due. The default sleeve split is 60% growth equities, 20% high-grade debt, 15% real assets, 5% satellite — educational, not advice. Indian savers should map PPF/NPS/ELSS inside Section 80C and verify POMIS joint-holding caps with India Post.
