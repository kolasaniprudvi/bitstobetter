HRA Exemption Rules

HRA exemption is calculated as the minimum of these three conditions:

  1. Actual HRA received from employer
  2. Actual rent paid minus 10% of basic salary
  3. 50% of basic salary for metro cities, 40% for non-metro cities

Metro Cities

Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai are considered metro cities for HRA calculation (50% of basic salary).
