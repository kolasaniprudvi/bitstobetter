Loan Pre-payment Benefits

Pre-paying your loan reduces the outstanding principal, which reduces the interest component. You can choose to either reduce your EMI amount or shorten the loan tenure.

Reduce Tenure vs Reduce EMI

  • Reduce Tenure: Higher interest savings, loan closes earlier
  • Reduce EMI: Lower monthly burden, more cash flow flexibility
