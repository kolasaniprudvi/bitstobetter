National Pension System (NPS)

NPS is a government-sponsored pension scheme that helps you save for retirement. At maturity (age 60), you must use at least 40% of the corpus to purchase an annuity for monthly pension.

Tax Benefits

  • 80CCD(1B): Additional ₹50,000 deduction over and above 80C
  • 80CCD(1): Up to 10% of salary (self-employed: 20%) deductible
