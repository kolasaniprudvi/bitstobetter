What is SWP?

Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) allows you to withdraw a fixed amount regularly from your mutual fund investments. It's ideal for creating regular income in retirement while keeping your money invested.

SWP Benefits

  • Regular Income: Fixed monthly withdrawals for expenses
  • Capital Appreciation: Remaining corpus continues to grow
  • Flexibility: Adjust withdrawal amount as needed
