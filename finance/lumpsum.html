Lumpsum Investment

Lumpsum investment means investing a large amount at once instead of small regular investments. It's ideal when you have a surplus amount like bonus, inheritance, or sale proceeds.

Lumpsum Formula

FV = P × (1 + r)^n

Where P = Principal amount, r = annual return rate, n = number of years
