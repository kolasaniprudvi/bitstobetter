Gratuity Calculation Formula

Gratuity is calculated based on your last drawn salary and years of service:

Under Payment of Gratuity Act

Gratuity = (Last Drawn Salary × 15 × Years of Service) / 26

Not Covered under Act

Gratuity = (Last Drawn Salary × 15 × Years of Service) / 30

Tax Exemption

Gratuity is tax-free up to ₹20 lakh under Section 10(10) of the Income Tax Act. Amount above ₹20 lakh is taxable.
