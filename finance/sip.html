What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a way to invest in mutual funds by investing a fixed amount regularly. It helps in building wealth over time through the power of compounding and rupee cost averaging.

SIP Formula

FV = P × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] × (1 + r)

Where P = SIP amount, r = expected monthly return, n = number of months
