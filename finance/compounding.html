Power of Compounding

Compounding is the process where your investment earns returns, and those returns earn more returns. Over time, this creates exponential growth.

The Rule of 72

To estimate how long it takes to double your money, divide 72 by the annual return rate. At 12% return, money doubles in approximately 6 years (72/12).

Key Parameters

  • ● Consistency: Regular and disciplined inputs historically outperform sporadic attempts at timing the market.
  • ● Inflation: Always factor in a conservative inflation rate when looking at long-term future values to determine true purchasing power.

Myth vs. Fact

The Myth

"I need a massive starting amount to see real results."

The Fact

Starting early with small amounts usually beats starting late with large amounts due to the exponential nature of compounding over time.
