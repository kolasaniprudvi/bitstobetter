Power of Compounding

Compounding is the process where your investment earns returns, and those returns earn more returns. Over time, this creates exponential growth.

The Rule of 72

To estimate how long it takes to double your money, divide 72 by the annual return rate. At 12% return, money doubles in approximately 6 years (72/12).
