National Savings Certificate (NSC)

NSC is a government-backed savings instrument that offers guaranteed returns. The interest is compounded annually and reinvested, giving you compound growth.

NSC Features

  • Tenure: 5 years (fixed)
  • Interest Rate: 7.7% (compounded yearly)
  • Tax Benefit: Section 80C deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh
  • Risk: Sovereign guarantee (zero risk)
