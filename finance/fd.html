Strategic Execution

While FDs are safe, they are often outpaced by inflation. Strategic deployment is key to maximizing their utility.

Key Parameters

  • ● Laddering: Instead of locking all your money in a single 5-year FD, split it across 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5-year FDs. This provides regular liquidity.
  • ● Taxation: Interest earned is added to your taxable income. Be aware of your marginal slab rate to calculate true post-tax returns.

Myth vs. Fact

The Myth

"FD returns are completely tax-free."

The Fact

FD interest is fully taxable at your marginal slab rate, unless you use a specific 5-year Tax Saving FD under Section 80C.
