Understanding FIRE

What is FIRE?

FIRE = Financial Independence, Retire Early. It's the strategy of accumulating enough wealth to live off investment returns alone, without needing a salary. Instead of working until 60-65, FIRE proponents retire in their 40s or 50s.

✓ The 4% Safe Withdrawal Rate

Research by William Bengen (Trinity Study) shows that withdrawing 4% of your portfolio annually has a 95% success rate over 30+ years. Example: If you have ₹1 Cr, you can withdraw ₹4 lakhs/year safely.

⚠️ Critical: Inflation Matters

At 5% inflation, your purchasing power halves every 14 years. Our calculator factors in inflation to ensure your withdrawal amount (and lifestyle) stays constant in real terms, not nominal.

Common FIRE Pitfalls

❌ Ignoring Sequence-of-Returns Risk

Poor market returns in early retirement years can devastate your corpus. Maintain a 2-3 year cash buffer.

❌ Underestimating Healthcare Costs

Retirement healthcare (post-60) can spike unexpectedly. Budget 15-20% extra for medical expenses.

❌ Lifestyle Inflation

Your expenses often rise with income. Plan for this by reviewing your assumptions annually.

❌ Neglecting Tax Optimization

In retirement, strategically withdraw from tax-efficient accounts (PPF, ELSS before SIPs).
