What is Step-Up SIP?

Step-Up SIP allows you to increase your SIP amount by a fixed percentage every year. As your income grows, you can proportionally increase your investments, leading to significantly higher corpus over time.

Benefits of Step-Up SIP

  • Disciplined Investing: Auto-increase keeps pace with income growth
  • Higher Corpus: Compounding effect on increasing amounts
  • Inflation Hedge: Investments grow faster than inflation
