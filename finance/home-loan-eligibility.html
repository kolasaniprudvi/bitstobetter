Home Loan Eligibility Factors

Lenders use FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) to determine your loan eligibility. Typically, FOIR should be 50-60% of your monthly income.

Eligibility Formula

Max EMI = (Monthly Income × FOIR%) - Existing EMIs

Max Loan = Max EMI × [(1+r)^n - 1] / [r(1+r)^n]
