1. How Browser-Based Calculators Work

Unlike traditional web apps, BitsToBetter calculators execute entirely on your computer. Here's the flow:

User Input → Your Browser's JavaScript → Local Calculation
❌ No server request sent
❌ No data stored on our servers
❌ No third-party trackers receive your numbers

🧠 Why This Matters

Your salary, loan details, investment plans, and fitness metrics are nobody else's business. They stay on your device—encrypted in your browser's memory, and cleared when you close the tab or browser.

Technical detail: We use JavaScript's DOM API to read form inputs, compute results using industry-standard financial/fitness formulas, and render outputs—all without making HTTP requests to any backend.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

❓ Myth: BitsToBetter uses cookies to track you

False. We use optional, non-tracking cookies only for:

  • • Session Preferences: Remember your selected calculator category (auto-clears on logout)
  • • Dark/Light Mode: Your theme preference (stored in browser memory only)
  • • Saved Calculations (Opt-In): If you explicitly save a calculation, it's stored locally in your browser—not on our servers

✓ Fact: You Can Disable Cookies Anytime

Go to your browser settings → Privacy → Cookies → Block All. BitsToBetter will still work. You'll just need to re-select your theme each session.

⚙️ Technical: LocalStorage

Some calculators (like FIRE Engine) use browser localStorage to save your preferences between sessions. This is 100% local—never synced to our servers. You can clear it anytime via Browser DevTools → Application → Local Storage → Delete.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR & Privacy Laws

If you're in the EU, India, or any privacy-forward jurisdiction:

  • 📋 Right to Access: We don't store your data, so there's nothing to access. But contact us if you have questions.
  • 🗑️ Right to Deletion: Close your browser tab and your calculations vanish. No traces on our servers.
  • 📤 Right to Data Portability: Export your saved calculations (if using the optional feature) as JSON or CSV anytime.
  • 🚫 Right to Opt-Out of Cookies: Disable cookies in browser settings. BitsToBetter works fine without them.

Data Retention Policy

  • • Calculator Inputs: Cleared immediately when you refresh or close the tab
  • • Contact Form Submissions: Stored for 6 months, then auto-deleted
  • • Error Logs: Retained for 30 days for debugging, then discarded
  • • Cookies: Auto-expire based on your browser's session settings (max 1 year)
