The Power of Compound Habits

Small daily actions compound over time to create remarkable results. Just as compound interest grows your wealth exponentially, compound habits grow your capabilities and achievements.

📈 The 1% Rule

Improving by just 1% every day results in being 37x better by the end of a year. Conversely, declining by 1% daily leads to near zero. Small consistent actions matter immensely.

How to Use This Tracker

  1. Add habits: Start with 3-5 simple, specific habits you want to build
  2. Check daily: Mark each habit as complete every day you do it
  3. Build streaks: Maintain consistency to build momentum and motivation
  4. Review weekly: Use the weekly overview to identify patterns and improve

Habit Building Best Practices

  • Start small: Begin with habits that take less than 2 minutes
  • Be specific: Define exactly what completing the habit means
  • Stack habits: Attach new habits to existing routines
  • Track visually: Use this tracker to make progress visible
  • Never miss twice: If you miss a day, get back on track immediately
