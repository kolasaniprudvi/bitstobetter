Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to BitsToBetter! We know legal jargon can be confusing, so we’ve written our Privacy Policy to be as clear, warm, and human-readable as possible.

The TL;DR: All your financial calculations happen safely on your own device. We never see your salary, loan amounts, or savings data. It stays with you!

1. What Information Do We Actually Collect?

We strongly believe in collecting as little as possible. When you use our calculators (like the FIRE Engine or EMI tool), none of the numbers you type are sent to our servers. Your data lives in your browser and disappears when you leave or clear your cache.

Here is what we do collect to keep the site running smoothly:

  • Basic Technical Data: Things like what browser you are using, your general location (like country or city), and how fast our pages load for you. This helps us fix bugs and make the site faster.
  • Contact Information: If you send us an email to say hi or report an issue, we'll see your email address and whatever you write to us.

2. Let's Talk About Cookies & Ads (Google AdSense)

BitsToBetter is completely free to use. To keep the lights on, pay for our servers, and keep building great tools, we show advertisements.

We use Google AdSense to show these ads. Google and its partners use small text files called "cookies" to figure out what ads might be relevant to you based on your visit to this site or other sites on the Internet.

You Have Control Over Your Ads

If you prefer not to see personalized ads, that is completely fine! You can easily opt out by visiting Google Ads Settings or the aboutads.info portal.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

We only use the data we collect to:

  • Make sure the website is fast, secure, and working properly.
  • Understand which calculators are the most popular so we know what to build next.
  • Display relevant advertising to keep the platform free.
  • Reply to your questions if you reach out to us.

4. Your Privacy Rights

No matter where you live, we believe you should have the right to your data. If you are in the UK, Europe (GDPR), or regions with similar laws, you have specific rights to access, update, or delete your information.

Since we don't save your calculator data in the first place, there isn't much for us to delete! But if you ever want to chat about privacy or exercise your rights regarding any emails you've sent us, just email us at privacy@bitstobetter.com.

5. A Quick Note on External Links

Sometimes we link out to helpful resources, research papers, or external websites. Once you click those links and leave BitsToBetter, their privacy policies take over. We always recommend giving them a quick read!
