Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026 · Effective for bitstobetter.com

BitsToBetter (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website https://bitstobetter.com (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices — including disclosures required for Google AdSense participation and similar third-party advertising networks.

1. Information we collect

Calculator inputs. Loan amounts, interest rates, FIRE assumptions, and similar values you enter into our tools are processed entirely in your browser. We do not transmit calculator payloads to our servers as part of the computation pipeline.

Log and device data. Like most websites, our hosting edge (Cloudflare Pages) may automatically receive technical data such as IP address, browser type, referrer, and request timestamps for security, availability, and abuse prevention.

Voluntary contact data. If you email us or use a contact form, we receive the details you choose to send (for example name, email address, and message content).

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or the industry opt-out portal at aboutads.info/choices. For EEA/UK users, Google may rely on consent frameworks; you can manage preferences via the consent interface presented on our site when deployed.

Third parties, including Google, may place and read cookies on your browser or use web beacons to collect information as a result of ad serving. Learn more in Google’s Advertising Technologies and Google Privacy Policy.

If we enable privacy-conscious analytics (for example Cloudflare Web Analytics or a consent-gated analytics product), we use aggregated traffic metrics to improve performance and content quality. Where cookies are not required, we prefer cookieless measurement.

Where the GDPR / UK GDPR applies, we process personal data on the basis of: (a) legitimate interests in running a secure informational and calculator website; (b) consent where required for non-essential cookies and personalised advertising; and (c) performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps when you contact us for support.

5. Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Server logs are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for security and operations. Email correspondence is retained for the period needed to resolve your request. We apply industry-standard technical measures at the edge (TLS, CDN hardening); no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Calculators on BitsToBetter are educational utilities. Outputs are not investment, tax, or insurance advice. Because computation is client-side, your scenario assumptions stay on your device unless you voluntarily export a PDF or share screenshots. PDF generation via jsPDF occurs locally in the browser.
